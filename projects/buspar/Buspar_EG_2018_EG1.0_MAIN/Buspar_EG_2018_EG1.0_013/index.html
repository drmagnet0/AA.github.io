Dosage:15

The initial dose
10–15 mg/day in 2 or 3 divided doses
The usual therapeutic dose
15–30 mg/day in 2 or 3 divided doses

The initial dose may be increased every 2 to 3 days.
The maximum recommended dose is 45 mg in divided doses.

For the 10-mg tablet:

10-mg tablet is divided into 2 parts; each is 5 mg.
1 tablet and half are taken per day on 3 divided parts

For the 15-mg tablet:

15-mg tablet is divided into 2 parts; each is 7.5 mg, taken 2 times per day or
the tablet is divided into 3 parts; each is 5 mg, taken 3 times per day.
